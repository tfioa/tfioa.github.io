The Fault in our Algorithms is a global youth-led responsible technology initiative that is shaping the future of equitable technology.
We raise awareness about the impact of bias in technology, and advocate for the use of responsible and ethical AI to help address social issues such as poverty, inequality, and discrimination.
Our podcast provides a platform for young people to share their unique and diverse experiences and perspectives on the use of technology in their daily lives.
